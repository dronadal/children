INVEST IN A CHILD’S LIFE. Your generous donation combined with other donors will help individuals with developmental disabilities achieve their full potential.

Types Of Donations

Annual Appeal

You will directly impact programming by donating to our annual appeal campaign. Direct your donation to a specific activity, event, or designate as unrestricted.

Summer Camp Sponsorship

Each donation furthers our mission . . . to enhance the learning and enrich the lives of people with special needs.

Memorial Gifts

Memorial gifts can be made to honor a loved one. Children’s Dispensary will send an acknowledgement of your gift to the person or family you request.

Sponsor an Event

We are always seeking corporate sponsorship and volunteers at special events. Have fun and support Children’s Dispensary at the same time by your participation.

Other Ways To Donate

Planned Giving

Consider the tax advantages of including Children’s Dispensary in your will or estate plan. Gifts of stock real estate, or personal property in addition to cash and endowed gifts are accepted.

In-Kind Gifts

The programs of Children’s Dispensary often have needs of in-kind items. Contact our administrative office for more information.

Matching Gifts

Double your gift to Children’s Dispensary with a matching gift from your employer. If your company participates in a matching gift program please consider this option. Contact your company’s human resource or community relations department to see if matching gifts are available.
